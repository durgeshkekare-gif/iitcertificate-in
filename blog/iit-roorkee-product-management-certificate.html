IIT Roorkee Strategic Product Management Certificate 2026 — Full Review

What makes the IIT Roorkee Product Management program unique

The Post Graduate Certificate in Strategic Product Management from IIT Roorkee's Continuing Education Centre stands out for three features: (1) a Build Your Own Product (BYOP) initiative where participants develop real-world solutions under mentorship, (2) mandatory campus immersion at IIT Roorkee's 175+ year-old campus in Roorkee, and (3) hands-on training in industry-standard tools — JIRA, Figma and Google Analytics.

Curriculum highlights

Product strategy and user research; analytics and data-driven product decisions; AI integration in product management; business models and GTM strategies; product roadmaps; agile methodologies; stakeholder management; A/B testing and metrics; case studies from Indian and global product companies.

Who should apply

Best for: product managers with 1–5 years of experience targeting senior PM or Lead PM roles; aspiring product managers in software or tech companies; engineers transitioning to product roles who want both an IIT credential and practical product tools training.

IIT Roorkee vs IIM Indore CPOP

IIM Indore's CPOP (Chief Product Officer Programme) is the IIM equivalent for product managers — but it requires 8+ years of experience and costs ₹4–5L. The IIT Roorkee program is more accessible (lower experience requirement, ₹1.5L fee) and more hands-on with the BYOP project. Both are strong credentials. Choose CPOP if you are targeting CPO roles with 8+ years; choose IIT Roorkee if you are mid-career and want the combination of IIT credential + practical tools + campus experience.
