IIT Madras GenAI & LLMs — what you actually learn

The IIT Madras Advanced Certificate in GenAI & LLMs covers the full production GenAI stack: LLM architecture (transformers, attention), Prompt Engineering, RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) system design, Fine-tuning models (Llama, Mistral), LangChain for AI agents, HuggingFace ecosystem, vector databases (Pinecone, Chroma), and deployment. This is the curriculum for someone who wants to actually build GenAI applications — not just use them.

IIT Roorkee Applied DS & AI — what you actually learn

The IIT Roorkee PG Certificate in Applied DS & AI gives a comprehensive data science foundation: Python, statistics, supervised/unsupervised ML, deep learning (TensorFlow/PyTorch), NLP, Computer Vision, data engineering (SQL, big data basics), and model deployment. Better for someone who wants to become a data scientist across a wide range of problems rather than specialising in GenAI.
