IIT Bombay CTO Programme 2026 — Complete Review: Fee, Curriculum and Who Should Apply

What is the IIT Bombay CTO Programme?

The Chief Technology Officer Programme from IIT Bombay's Shailesh J. Mehta School of Management (SJMSOM) is a 1-year executive certificate program for senior technology professionals targeting C-suite roles. It runs as weekend live online sessions with two campus immersion modules at IIT Bombay campus. Fee: approximately ₹4–5L + GST.

SJMSOM at IIT Bombay (NIRF #3 Engineering, NIRF #4 Overall) is one of India's most respected business and management schools within a technical institution. The CTO credential from IIT Bombay carries significant weight in technology leadership circles in India and internationally.

Who is the CTO Programme designed for?

The programme targets senior technology professionals in the following profiles: Vice Presidents of Engineering, Heads of Technology, Senior Software Architects, Engineering Directors and technology professionals who are 3–5 years away from a CTO role. Minimum experience expectation is typically 8–12 years. Professionals from both product companies and IT services firms are eligible.

Curriculum — what you will learn

The CTO Programme curriculum covers the full technology leadership stack. Core modules include: technology strategy and vision-setting, AI and machine learning for technology leaders, cloud architecture and digital infrastructure, product management at scale, cybersecurity strategy and risk governance, digital transformation leadership, platform economics and two-sided markets, technology P&L management, and organisational design for engineering teams.

Two campus immersion modules at IIT Bombay bring cohort members together for case studies, leadership simulations and faculty-led deep dives. These are among the most valued aspects of the programme by alumni.

IIT Bombay CTO vs IIT Kanpur CDAO — which is right for you?

Both are senior leadership programmes from top IITs. The key difference: the IIT Bombay CTO programme focuses on technology strategy, engineering leadership and building technology organisations — ideal for professionals managing large engineering teams or product technology. The IIT Kanpur CDAO programme focuses on digital transformation and AI strategy at an organisational level — ideal for professionals leading enterprise-wide digital and AI initiatives regardless of whether they manage engineers directly.

If your role is primarily about leading technology teams and making technology architecture decisions → IIT Bombay CTO. If your role is about leading digital transformation and AI adoption across a business → IIT Kanpur CDAO.

Is the IIT Bombay CTO programme worth ₹4–5L?

For professionals genuinely targeting a CTO role within 3–5 years, the IIT Bombay credential at ₹4–5L is justified. The programme provides three things that are hard to get elsewhere at this price point: the IIT Bombay institutional credential (NIRF #4 overall), a peer cohort of senior technology professionals, and structured access to IIT Bombay faculty and campus. These are career-long assets. The break-even on the investment typically happens within 12–18 months of completing the programme through salary or role upgrades.
