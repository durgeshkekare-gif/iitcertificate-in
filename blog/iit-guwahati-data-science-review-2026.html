E&ICT Academy — what it means and why it matters

The E&ICT (Electronics and ICT) Academy is a consortium of 9 IITs funded by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Govt of India. IIT Guwahati is one of the lead institutes. This means: the curriculum is reviewed by IIT faculty, the certificate carries both the IIT Guwahati name and E&ICT Academy certification, and the programme is backed by government mandate — not just a commercial ed-tech arrangement. At ~₹1.5L, this is excellent value for an IIT + Govt of India backed credential.
