IIT Certificate Programs 2026 — The Complete Guide for Working Professionals

What are IIT certificate programs for working professionals?

IIT certificate programs are executive education offerings from the Indian Institutes of Technology — specifically structured for working professionals who cannot pursue full-time degrees. They require no JEE or GATE, run on weekends, and result in a certificate issued directly by the IIT. Some include campus immersion; most are delivered online with live faculty sessions.

How to choose the right program

For technology leaders (CTO/CIO track): IIT Bombay CTO Programme (1 year, campus immersions) or IIT Delhi CTO Programme (9 months, online). IIT Kanpur's CDAO program is best for Chief Digital & AI Officer targets.

For general management and strategy: IIT Delhi Advanced Strategic Management (9 months). Most accessible program from a top-3 IIT — ₹2L + GST.

For product managers: IIT Roorkee Strategic Product Management (5 months) — includes Build Your Own Product (BYOP), campus module, and industry tools (JIRA, Figma).

For SCM/operations professionals: IIT Bombay Global Supply Chain Management & Transformation (6 months) — from the SJM School of Management.

Important: programs not available via enrollment partner

IIT Bombay's Investment Strategies & Portfolio Management program and IIT Kharagpur's VGSOM programs are not currently available via enrollment partner. You must apply directly via the respective institution's website. This does not affect the program quality or certificate — it only means there is no third-party support for applications or EMI.

Fees at a glance

Fees range from ₹80,000 (IIT Delhi Brand Management) to ₹4.2L (IIT Kanpur CDAO) per program. Most programs are ₹1.5–2L + GST. The IIT Bombay CTO Programme is the most expensive at ₹4–5L for a 1-year blended program.
