IIT Delhi CEP Programs 2026 — All 4 Certificates Compared

IIT Delhi's executive education — CEP

IIT Delhi's Continuing Education Programme (CEP), run through the Department of Management Studies, offers 4 executive certificate programs for working professionals in 2026. IIT Delhi was the first IIT to launch structured online certificate programs under eVIDYA@IITD. One important note: CEP programs at IIT Delhi are e-certificate only — no alumni status, no placement services. The institutional value of the IIT Delhi certificate itself remains strong.

Which IIT Delhi program should you choose?

CTO Programme: For technology leaders. Covers technology strategy, R&D management, innovation, digital transformation. 9 months, online. Minimum 5 years experience. Admission involves profile review and written statement by IIT Delhi.

Advanced Strategic Management: The most general-purpose program — suitable for managers in any function who want a strategic management credential from a top-3 IIT. 9 months, ~₹2L. 4+ years experience. Campus immersion included.

Brand Management: Most affordable at ₹80,000 + GST. 6 months, live Sundays. Designed for brand managers, marketers, PR professionals and founders. Capstone project required for completion certificate.

Healthcare Entrepreneurship & Management: Unique offering — for clinicians, biomedical engineers, healthcare entrepreneurs and researchers who want to build healthcare products and services. 5 months + 2-day campus visit at IIT Delhi. Minimum 60% score for completion certificate.

IIT Delhi CEP: what you should know before applying

Three things to be clear about: (1) Only e-certificates are issued — no physical certificate. (2) Alumni status is explicitly not granted by IIT Delhi CEP programs. (3) IIT Delhi is not involved in placement services — that is handled separately by the enrollment partner if applicable. None of this affects the educational value or the IIT Delhi institutional credential on your CV.
