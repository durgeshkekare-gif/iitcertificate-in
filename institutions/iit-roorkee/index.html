About IIT Roorkee

IIT Roorkee was founded in 1847 — the oldest technical institution in Asia. Their Strategic Product Management program stands out for its Build Your Own Product (BYOP) initiative, campus immersion at Roorkee campus, and hands-on use of industry tools (JIRA, Figma, Google Analytics). This practical orientation makes it one of the strongest product management credentials in India.
