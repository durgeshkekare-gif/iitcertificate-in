About IIT Delhi

IIT Delhi is NIRF #2 and QS #150. The CEP runs management programs through the Dept. of Management Studies — one of the few IITs with a strong management-focused executive education portfolio. IIT Delhi was the first IIT to launch online certificate programs under eVIDYA@IITD. Important note: CEP certificates are e-certificates only. Alumni status and placement services are not provided. The IIT Delhi certificate itself carries strong institutional value.
