Frequently asked questions

No. All executive certificate programs listed here are open to working professionals based on work experience and educational background alone. No JEE, GATE or any entrance exam is required. Admission is typically based on a profile review by the respective IIT department.
Programs marked "View program ↗" are available through an enrollment partner for application support, EMI, career services and student support. Programs marked "Enroll directly via IIT" (like IIT Kharagpur) are offered directly by the institution — you apply through their official website. The IIT certificate is identical in both cases; the enrollment partner only handles the student journey, not the academic content.
It varies by program. IIT Bombay CTO includes 2 campus immersions. IIT Roorkee's Product Management program has a mandatory campus module. IIT Delhi's Brand Management and Healthcare programs include a 2-day campus visit. Most programs are primarily online with optional or scheduled campus modules — check the specific program page for details.
This varies by IIT and program. IIT Delhi's CEP programs explicitly state that certificate programs do not provide alumni status. IIT Bombay's educational outreach programs may grant access to the alumni community. Check each specific program's terms carefully — "alumni status" and "certificate from IIT" are distinct, and the certificate itself carries the institutional value regardless.
